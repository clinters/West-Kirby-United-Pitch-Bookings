Data Deletion Instructions

If you wish to have your data removed from the West Kirby Pitch Bot system:

  1. Message the WhatsApp bot: “delete me” from your registered phone number; or
  2. Email admin@westkirbyunited.co.uk with your number and team.

We will remove your phone-to-team mapping and active bookings. Minimal log entries may be retained for up to 12 months for audit/security.
