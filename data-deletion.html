Data Deletion Instructions

If you wish to have your data removed from the West Kirby United Pitch Bot system, please follow the steps below:

Step 1: Contact Us

Send an email to wku.pitchbot@gmail.com or message the admin directly via WhatsApp.

Step 2: Verification

We may ask you to confirm your phone number or team affiliation to verify your identity before processing deletion.

Step 3: Removal

Once verified, we will remove your phone number and any associated bookings from our Google Sheets database within 7 days.
