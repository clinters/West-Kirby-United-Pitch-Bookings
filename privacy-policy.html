Privacy Policy – West Kirby Pitch Booking Bot

Last updated: 13 September 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how West Kirby Pitch Booking Bot (“we”, “us”) collects and uses information when team managers interact with our WhatsApp booking assistant and connected Google Sheets.

1. Who we are

Controller: West Kirby United Football Club
Contact: admin@westkirbyunited.co.uk

2. What we collect

  • WhatsApp phone number (digits only)
  • Manager name (if provided)
  • Team affiliation(s) (e.g., U7 Ajax)
  • Booking details (game week/date, pitch, slot, notes)
  • Basic WhatsApp webhook metadata (message type, timestamps, selections)

We do not read message content beyond the commands required to process bookings.

3. How we use your data

  • Link managers to teams
  • Provide availability, bookings, cancellations, and reviews
  • Maintain an audit log for disputes and transparency
  • Help club admins manage weekly pitch allocations

4. Legal bases (UK GDPR / GDPR)

  • Legitimate interests: football operations and scheduling
  • Consent: for optional WhatsApp notifications, if required

5. Sharing & processors

  • Meta (WhatsApp Business API) – to deliver/receive messages
  • Google (Sheets & Apps Script) – to store and process bookings
  • GitHub Pages – to host this policy website

We do not sell personal data.

6. International transfers

WhatsApp and Google services may transfer data outside the UK/EU. We rely on their safeguards (e.g., SCCs).

7. Data retention

  • Bookings & logs: retained up to 12 months after the season, then deleted or anonymised.
  • Team manager mapping: retained while you are an active manager or until you request deletion.

8. Your rights

You can request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your data.

To exercise rights: message the bot “delete me” from your registered number, or email admin@westkirbyunited.co.uk.

9. Children

This bot is for team managers and club officials, not children. We do not knowingly process children’s data directly.

10. Security

We limit access to Google Sheets, use role-based controls, and maintain logs. No system is 100% secure; please secure your own device.

11. Data deletion instructions

Send “delete me” to the bot or email admin@westkirbyunited.co.uk. See the Data Deletion page for details.

12. Changes

We may update this policy and will revise the “last updated” date accordingly.
