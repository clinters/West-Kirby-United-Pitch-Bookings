Privacy Policy

West Kirby United Pitch Bot ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our WhatsApp bot to manage football pitch bookings.

Information We Collect

We only collect minimal data: phone numbers (to identify team managers) and booking information (pitch, timeslot, gameweek, team ID). No sensitive or financial data is stored.

How We Use Information

Information is used solely to manage pitch bookings and avoid scheduling conflicts. Data is stored securely in Google Sheets linked to the bot.

Data Sharing

We do not share personal data with third parties. Access is limited to administrators of the West Kirby United Pitch Bot.

Data Retention

Booking data is kept for record-keeping during the season and may be cleared annually. Phone numbers remain linked to team IDs until you request removal.

Your Rights

You may request removal of your data at any time. See our Data Deletion Instructions.
