Terms of Service – West Kirby Pitch Booking Bot

Last updated: 13 September 2025

1. Purpose

The West Kirby Pitch Booking Bot (“Service”) helps team managers request, review, swap, and cancel junior football pitch bookings via WhatsApp, backed by Google Sheets.

2. Eligibility & Accounts

The Service is intended for authorised team managers and club officials. Access is linked to your WhatsApp phone number; do not share devices or credentials that could allow unauthorised use.

3. Acceptable Use

  • Provide accurate information when making bookings.
  • Respect other teams’ allocations; no spam or abusive language.
  • Follow admin instructions and club policies.

4. Bookings & Admin Overrides

All bookings are subject to availability and club approval. Administrators may modify or cancel bookings to resolve clashes, weather issues, or operational needs.

5. Limits

Per-team limits (e.g., one slot per game week) may be enforced. Automated checks may prevent duplicate bookings.
